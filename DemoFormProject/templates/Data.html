About the Data Base

My data is about Unemployment people around the world. The data shows the unemployment rate of the countries in the years 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013 and 2014. From the data I can infer which country can handle unemployment people.

About unemployment

Unemployment is when persons above the age of 15 are not in paid employment or self-employment and are currently available for work during a specific period. The effect of unemployed people around the world can cause stress, poverty and conflict. Economics Noble prise winner Robert J. Shiller said that rising inequality (between unemployed people and employed people) is the most important problem.
