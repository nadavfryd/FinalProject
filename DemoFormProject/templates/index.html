Unemployed people around the world

There are many people around the world that they are unemployed. People can be unemployed from several reasons: some of them can have physical disability and because of that they can't work, some of them can't find a job no matter what and some of them just don't want to work.

Unemployment is a big subject in the world. People are trying to reduce the rate of unemployed people in the world by publicize more jobs and to try giving training or career retraining in order to give a long term job for unemployed people.
